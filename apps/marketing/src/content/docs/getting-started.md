Rigel is a Mac app that connects to your Kubernetes clusters, reads them like a senior SRE, and hands you fixes you approve. It runs on your machine and talks to your clusters directly, so there's nothing to deploy or expose.

We're putting the finishing touches on it now.

> **Coming soon.** Leave your email on the [home page](/#cta) and we'll let you know the moment the macOS app is ready to download.

## What you'll need

* A Mac (Apple Silicon or Intel).
* A Kubernetes cluster and a kubeconfig on your machine, the same one `kubectl` uses.
* Optional: an AI key for the chat copilot. You bring your own; the read and observe panels work without it.

## What it does

While you wait, the rest of these docs cover what Rigel does once it's running, from the AI chat and the autonomous agent to the app catalog and every resource panel. Have a look around.
